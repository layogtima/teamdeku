About SillyCuts

SillyCuts is your friendly neighborhood laser-cutting service. Send a vector file, get an instant rupee quote, and we cut it clean.

What we cut

Acrylic (clear & black), birch plywood, MDF, cardboard, leather, and anodized tags — 1 to 12 mm thick.

How it works

Drop a DXF, see it in 3D on the bed, pick quantity & turnaround, then confirm — your quote (with a summary card) lands on our WhatsApp.

Turnaround

From ~24 hours to 3 days, depending on how soon you need it. Every file is reviewed by hand before cutting.
