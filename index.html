Our Manifesto

Balu has been making and breaking things since he was nine. Not for fun. For science. He's been walking the lanes of SP Road since he was eleven, not to shop, but to hunt. Hunting for the perfect capacitor, the most reliable resistor, the switch with the most satisfying click.

At twenty-eight, that obsession hasn't faded. It's been refined. Forged. Turned into Deku.

This is not a hobby. It's a compulsion to build things that work, and work flawlessly. We don't chase trends. We don't cut corners. We build cool shit, period. The kind of shit you'll still be using years from now.

And Nami, the two-year-old Golden Retriever? She's the CEO of Quality Control. If it doesn't survive her tail wags, it doesn't ship.
